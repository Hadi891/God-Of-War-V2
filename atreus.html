Atreus

Atreus is the teenage demigod son of Kratos and the giantess Laufey. He is known to the giants and Aesir as “Loki.” As Ragnarök approaches, Atreus is desperate to explore and better understand his identity and find his place in the world. He has trained hard with his father and while their relationship remains strong, Atreus is eager to begin forging a path of his own.
